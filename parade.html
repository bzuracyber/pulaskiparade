2026 Parade Theme

"For Our Freedom and Yours" — General Kościuszko & General Pulaski, Soldiers of Liberty for Poland and America — Saluting 250 Years of American Independence

The 89th Annual Pulaski Day Parade marches up 5th Avenue in the heart of New York City, celebrating the contributions of Polish-Americans and honoring the legacy of General Casimir Pulaski, who gave his life for American freedom.

This year's parade proudly salutes 250 years of American Independence and the enduring bond between Poland and the United States.
