Why Join

Be Part of Something Great

The GPMPC is one of the most important Polish-American organizations in New York City.

🏛️

Historic Organization

Founded in 1937, the GPMPC has organized the annual Pulaski Day Parade for nearly 90 years, making it one of the longest-running ethnic parades in New York City.

🇵🇱

Polish Heritage

Celebrate and preserve the contributions of Polish-Americans to the United States, honoring General Pulaski's sacrifice for American freedom.

🤝

Community Connection

Build lasting connections with the Polish-American community across the greater New York metropolitan area and beyond.
