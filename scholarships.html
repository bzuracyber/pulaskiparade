Scholarships

Supporting the next generation of Polish-American students.

Education & Heritage

GPMPC Scholarship Program

The General Casimir Pulaski Memorial Parade Committee is committed to supporting Polish-American students through scholarship opportunities.

Scholarship Information

For the most current information about available scholarships, eligibility requirements, and application deadlines, please contact the GPMPC directly at events@pulaskiparade.org or attend a committee meeting.

Academic Scholarships

The GPMPC supports Polish-American students pursuing higher education. Scholarships recognize academic achievement and commitment to Polish-American heritage.

Miss Polonia Program

Each year, local committees select Miss Polonia and Junior Miss Polonia representatives who march in the parade and represent their communities throughout the season.

Cultural Awards

Awards recognizing outstanding contributions to Polish-American culture, heritage, and community service are presented at the annual banquet.

Polish Schools Support

The GPMPC supports Polish supplementary schools throughout the metropolitan area, including Polish Falcons Nest 946 and other educational institutions.

Related Organizations

Scholarship-Offering Partners

Polish & Slavic Federal Credit Union (PSFCU)

PSFCU offers scholarship programs for Polish-American students. Visit psfcu.com for more information.

The Kościuszko Foundation

The Kościuszko Foundation provides scholarships and cultural exchange programs for Polish and American students. A 2026 parade sponsor.

Marie Skłodowska Curie Professional Women's Association

Annual Dinner Dance & Scholarship Awards — celebrating outstanding Polish-American women and supporting student achievement.

Centrala Polskich Szkół Dokształcających

Organization of Polish supplementary schools supporting Polish language and heritage education. Visit centralapolskichszkol.org

For more information about scholarships and educational programs, contact us.
